

Ingredients

Okok leaves (cleaned and chopped) — about 500 g or adapted for 4-6 servings

Smoked beef (or beef + smoked meat) — ~200 g, cut into bite-sized pieces

Smoked fish — 1–2 pieces (depending on size)

Crayfish or small dried shrimp (optional)

Peanut paste (or ground roasted peanuts) — ~200 g

Palm oil (red/“huile rouge”) — ~100 ml

Palm pulp/juice (or palm nut cream) — as available

Salt — to taste

Water — enough to cook the sauce and leaves

(Optional) Chili, onions, seasoning cubes, garlic, depending on taste
