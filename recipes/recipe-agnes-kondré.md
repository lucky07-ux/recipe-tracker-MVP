Ingredients (for ~4-6 servings)

1 kg green (unripe) plantains — peeled and cut into large chunks

500 g – 1 kg meat (goat meat preferred, or beef/pork)

1 large onion, chopped

2-3 tomatoes, chopped

Garlic (about 5-8 cloves), minced

Ginger (≈ 2-4 Tbsp fresh, grated)

Fresh herbs: basil or parsley, celery/leek as desired

Spices: white pepper, black pepper, thyme, bay leaf / bouquet garni

Traditional West-Cameroonian spices: nutmeg (pébé seeds), djansang (akpi) seeds, rondelle slices, quatre-côtés if available.
