Chicken

1 kg chicken pieces (drumsticks, wings, breasts, or thighs)

Marinade

3 cloves garlic (crushed)

1 tsp ginger (grated)

1 tsp white/black pepper

1 tsp paprika (optional)

1 Maggi cube (or 1 tsp chicken bouillon)

Salt (to taste)

Juice of 1 lemon (optional but recommended)

1 tbsp mustard (optional)

2 tbsp vegetable oil

Breading

2–3 eggs (beaten)

1–2 cups flour

1–2 cups bread crumbs
If you have no breadcrumbs, crush biscuits or cornflakes.

Frying

Oil for deep frying
