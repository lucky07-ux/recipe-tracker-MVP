Main Ingredients

2–3 cups corn (maïs frais) — grated or roughly crushed
(If you don’t have fresh corn, you can use canned corn but drain well.)

2 cups cassava leaves (feuilles de manioc), finely pounded

2–3 ripe plantains (bananes mûres) — cut into small cubes

Palm Base

2–3 cups palm nut juice (jus de noix de palme)
Or substitute with 150–200 ml palm oil diluted with a little water.

Seasonings

1 Maggi cube (optional)

Salt (to taste)
