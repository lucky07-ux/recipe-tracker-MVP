5. Yorkshire Pudding
Ingredients: Flour, eggs, milk, oil.
Steps:
Preheat muffin tray with a little oil in the oven.
Mix flour, eggs, and milk into a smooth batter.
Pour batter into hot tray and bake at 220°C (425°F) for 20–25 minutes 
until puffed.
Tip: Serve with roast beef and gravy for a classic Sunday meal.
